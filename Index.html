To experience and document.

My name is Greg DiGalbo, and I am currently a student at Virginia Tech studyng Professional and Technical writing. I will be graduating with the class of 2017, and hope to pursue a career in motocross journalism. I originally came into Virginia Tech as a Fisheries Science student, but soon realized that my real passion was in writing.During this time my interest in the sport of motocross was increasing, and I started to become interested in the idea of Motocross Journalism. This has developed into a borderline obsession, checking every MX news source multiple times daily. I hope to be able to use my passion for the sport, as well as my education, to inform others and report the most current news. While I do this, I also hope to learn more about the sport myself, as well as all forms of media.

I have created this webpage with the intention of using it as a glimpse into my life and my professional qualifications.
